Mario Alejandro Leiva

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Universidad Nacional del Sur in Buenos Aires, Argentina, where my research focused on probabilistic structured argumentation in the field of cybersecurity. Currently, I work as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Universidad Nacional del Sur. In addtion, I am currently pursuing a specialization in Data Science. In this role, I actively engage in cutting-edge research, contribute as a Teaching Assistant in various computer science courses, and further enhance my expertise in data science. My interest lie in the areas of argumentation systems, cybersecurity, applied research, software development, and the application of logic programming to solve real-world problems. I have a strong passion for expanding the frontiers of computer science, conducting applied research, and creating software.
